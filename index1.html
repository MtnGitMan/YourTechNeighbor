ABOUT

The gap between "call the store" and "call an IT department."

Most tech problems don't fit neatly into one category. Your security cameras won't talk to your Wi-Fi. Your dad's old home movies are trapped on tapes nobody makes players for anymore. Your work-from-home setup needs a real network, not a router shoved in a closet. A big electronics chain won't take the job, and a managed IT company won't return your call for something this size.

That's the gap I work in. Computer science background, hands-on with hardware, and genuinely interested in figuring out the project you didn't think anyone could help with — including specialty jobs most repair shops won't attempt. If you've got an idea and no idea how to build it, that's exactly the kind of call I want.
